
Goal: Complete the room ["My friend went on holiday in 2025 and shared some photos, but I haven’t heard from him since. Can you help me track him down for the police report?"]


Link: https://tryhackme.com/room/missingperson


1. Q1: What is the commercial name of this circuit?
   
   A1: Download the task files, extract the .zip, and use a reverse image search tool to find the location.

2. Q2: When did the event take place?
   
   A2: Since the event could take place multiple times in different years, we need to find exactly which year this picture was taken. Using tools to find the metadata of the image (such as metadata2go.com on the web, we can ascertain the year. Then simply looking online for the dates that the event took place in that year, we can find the answer.

3. Q3: He told me he ate delicious Mexican food. What is the name of the restaurant?
   
   A3: Same as Q1.

4. Q4: At what time was this photo taken?
   
   A4: Same as Q2, we can find the original date as well as time at date_time_original.

5. Q5: He sent me a message, this is the last I heard from him: ”Went to this cool MotoGP after party, and became friends with one of the local DJs who played that night. We’re going to visit a cave tomorrow.” What is the full address of the bar’s location?
    
   A5: Look for Bars, Restos, etc near the circuit on Google Maps, soon enough you will find one that matches the description.

6. Q6: What is the DJ's stage name?
    
   A6: The trick is to dig into the bar's social media page.

7. Q7: After digging into the DJ's other online accounts, what cave does he take tourists to?
    
   A7: Simply looking up caves for tourists in the local area, we can get a pretty good idea of where our friend might have been led to.

8. Q8: What number did the DJ list for his tour business?
    
   A8: Looking into the DJ's other social media (specifically, Facebook/Meta) will tell you their contact details.

Finished!
